Hi there,

And welcome to my portfolio. My name is Luka (just like the song) and I'm originally from Zagreb, Croatia, recently moved to Malmo, Sweden.

For the last bunch of years (remember Macromedia/Adobe Flash, anyone?), I've been working on:

  • Web & graphic design
  • Branding
  • Wireframing
  • Frontend development (HTML + CSS)
  • Wordpress - styling themes and optimizing them for faster page load
  • Mailchimp - creating email templates and setting up campaigns

While working on website designs, my approach would be to research on the client's competition and trying to determine the target audience. Based on that I would make decisions about colors and visuals. It seemed like a logical thing to do.

I've learned a lot about Personas, different types of UX deliverables, the process of interviews while studying for UX Research specialization from Nielsen Norman Group. Whenever I can, I try to catch up with lessons from Interaction Design Foundation.

When I'm not near computer, you can find me taking on KOM's on Strava's bike and running segments. Setting goals, career wise or personal, forces us to be accountable, and to grow. With that in mind, after finishing my first marathon in Rome in 2021. and vEveresting in 2022., my next challenge is to complete the Ironman in 2023. ... and become fluent in Swedish.
