Employer Value Proposition

Website.

The Brief

Company needs Employer Value Proposition (EVP) website to present its values and culture to potential candidates.

Target audience are either skilled professionals or younger people fresh out of universities who want to grow in the areas of IT, Finance or Human Resources.

The website needs to reflect fun and transparent work environment, and communicate career growth opportunities.

Overview

Project
Hoss.hr
When
2022.

What did I do

Wordpress theme UI / UX design Photoshop mockup Project management Mailchimp template HTML + CSS Concept design

The Problems

Project goal is to achieve the following:

  • Present the People & Culture of the company

  • Present the verticals and career paths to potential hirees

  • Present the perks to the pontential hirees

  • Explain the hiring process to the candidates

The Process

  • High-Definition photoshop mockup for the Look & Feel. Once approved, having it done in Photoshop first, helped to quickly export the assets for Wordpress.

  • Building the site in Wordpress. Themify was used as a framework for the theme. I added some custom effects and css animations.

  • Human Resources department and Management helped to polish up the content for the website.

  • I thought it would be nice to have a similar theme for Employee's Newsletter so I made a Mailchimp template.

    Mailchimp template

The site was launched (no pun intended) on October 13th alongside company presentation at a regional job fair.

Next Steps

  • Surveys to gather opinions / suggestions about the EVP website from new candidates.
