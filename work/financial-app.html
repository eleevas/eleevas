Financial web app

Internal tool for the finance team.

Brief

A company has a financial department with an established workflow. The purpose of this project is to explore posibilities for optimization, and potentially introducing changes.

My role

I'll be working with a developer, a number of representatives of the financial team (end users), and a CEO of the company on this project.

I have to research, conduct workshops with users and stakeholders, gather their feedback, then proceed with building and testing wireframes, user journey, and finish up with the delivery of interactive HTML + CSS prototypes which will become the frontend of MVP.

The Problem

How can we use the data?

With the current workflow, the company doesn't know:

  • How much resources does a task cost?
  • Why a certain task took a certain amount of time?
  • How much of company resources does a certain client cost (are they're a feasible client)?
  • How to measure team performance (KPI's)?
The Problem

How can a change be introduced to the team?

Change is hard, people are typically hesitant to change, even when it's for their benefit. Based on the preliminary interview, the finance team is not enthusiastic about the software. They have an established workflow with which they are happy with.
