VisX is an in-house business suite. Its reports and tools are used by technical support staff, team leads, managers, CEO and clients.

The Problem(s)

Project goal is to solve the following:

  • For Customer Service agents
    Which tools can we provide for the staff and how can they see how they're doing as an individual and compared to their peers?

  • For Team Leads
    How can we help Team Leads be proactive, and successfully manage their teams?

  • For Managers

    How can we show the managers how their staff is doing in terms of KPI's and business goals?

  • For C-levels

    How can we enable the company owner and key decision makers to make the right decisions based on analytical data about their company and staff?

What can be improved?

  • UX workshops and user interviews could be conducted in order to test hypotheses.

  • Based on the results from the UX research, changes to current workflow could be implemented and re-tested.

  • Software could benefit from a refreshed UI, considering the advancements and wider adoption of CSS grid and flexbox systems.

  • Few users have expressed they'd prefer a light theme. An interface where users can create and share custom themes could be implemented. This interface could be used as means to create branded themes.

  • By interviewing the users, it should be investigated Why some reports are rarely used, or not used at all.
